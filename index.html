This course offers a comprehensive introduction to transcriptomics, emphasizing its role in molecular and evolutionary biology. Students will delve into RNA-Seq principles and applications, gene expression regulation, and transcriptomic data analysis using tools like FastQC, Salmon, and DESeq2. The course covers essential topics such as data preprocessing, statistical analysis, and functional annotation, providing hands-on experience with RNA-Seq data processing and differential gene expression identification. Students will also explore the broader applications of transcriptomics in biological research, with opportunities to integrate transcriptomic data into multi-omics studies.
In this site, you will find the course information and resources.
